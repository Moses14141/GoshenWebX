Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Goshen Web X, your privacy is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily and knowingly provide to us. This typically includes your name, email address, phone number, and any project details you share through our contact forms, quote requests, or direct communication via email or phone.

We may also receive limited information about third parties when it is directly related to the website development, SEO, or portfolio services you have requested from us.

We never collect personal data without your explicit consent.

How We Use the Information We Collect

We use the information you provide solely to deliver outstanding digital services. This includes project coordination, client support, sending project updates, processing invoices, and occasionally sharing relevant newsletters or service information. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with outside parties except when strictly necessary to complete the services you requested or when required by applicable law.

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze how visitors interact with our content. These tools help us improve site performance, content relevance, and overall user experience.

The information collected through cookies is anonymous and does not identify you personally. This data is shared with Google Analytics solely for reporting on traffic and engagement for https://goshenwebx.io.

Remarketing

We may use remarketing cookies to display relevant advertisements to previous visitors of our website across other sites on the internet. These ads are based solely on your past visits and browsing behavior on our site.

You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting the Google Ads Preference Manager.

Third-Party Websites and Links

Our website may contain links to external websites for your convenience. We do not control these third-party sites or their privacy practices, and we are not responsible for their content or data handling. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Our Commitment to Data Security

We maintain appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we collect. All sensitive transactions, including payments, occur on secure, encrypted servers using industry-standard SSL technology.

Payment Processing

We accept secure online payments through PayPal and Stripe. All credit card and payment data is processed directly by these trusted providers on their secure platforms. We do not store your full payment details on our servers.

Protecting Children’s Privacy

Goshen Web X is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our website and services are not designed for or directed at individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect or maintain information from anyone we know is under 13.

If we discover that a user under the age of 18 has submitted personal information, we will promptly delete it and will not use it for any purpose.

Your Consent and Policy Updates

By using our website and services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date and post the revised version on this page. Your continued use after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

You are not required to provide personal information to browse our site. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us in writing. We will respond to your request as quickly as possible and in accordance with applicable law.

Contacting Goshen Web X

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at info@goshenwebx.io
