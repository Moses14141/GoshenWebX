How can we help you?

Search or browse our help topics

How do I change my password?

Go to My Account → Change Password. Enter your current password and new one.

How do I publish my website?

Open any site in the editor and click the big green Publish Site button.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! After publishing, go to My Sites → Connect Domain (coming soon).

How do I add my logo and change colors?

In the editor, click any section and use the right sidebar to upload images or pick colors.
