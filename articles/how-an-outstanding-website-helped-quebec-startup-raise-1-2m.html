CASE STUDY • OCTOBER 18, 2025 • QUEBEC

How an Outstanding Website Helped This Quebec Startup Raise $1.2 Million

True story + 7 practical lessons every small business can apply today

In October 2025, a young Quebec City startup specializing in online reservations for independent restaurants closed a $1.2 million seed round. The product was excellent and the team passionate… but what really made the difference during the investor pitch was their website.

The Story of ReserveLocal

Founded in 2024 by two Quebec entrepreneurs, ReserveLocal helps small restaurants manage reservations without paying the high commissions of big platforms. After 14 months of development, they were ready to raise funds to accelerate growth.

Their first site, built with an AI tool, looked nice… but it was slow, generic, and not very convincing. Investors would quickly scroll past it without being impressed.

The Turning Point: A Complete Website Redesign

In June 2025 they hired Goshen Web X. In just 6 weeks we delivered a fully custom website that included:

  • Lightning-fast performance (100/100 Lighthouse score)
  • Smooth, elegant micro-interactions
  • Modern design that perfectly reflected the brand
  • Powerful storytelling right from the homepage
  • An interactive live demo of the product built into the site

Pitch Day: The Website Stole the Show

During the final presentation to a Montreal venture fund, the founders opened their website instead of using a traditional PowerPoint. Investors were able to:

  • Book a fake table in 8 seconds
  • Experience the fluid micro-interactions
  • Instantly understand the product value

The lead partner said: “This isn’t just a reservation tool — it’s an experience I would actually want to use myself.”

Two weeks later the $1.2 million term sheet was signed.

7 Lessons You Can Apply Today

1. Your website is your best silent salesperson

It works 24/7 — even while you sleep.

2. Speed = Trust

A slow site turns off investors as much as customers.

3. Micro-interactions create emotion

They turn a tool into a memorable experience.

Final Takeaways for Entrepreneurs

A great website is not an expense — it’s a strategic asset that increases your company’s valuation and helps you close funding faster. It pays for itself many times over.
